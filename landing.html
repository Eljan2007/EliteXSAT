Learn How to Control your SAT Score

Here is how EliteXSAT puts your score in your hands:

Digital SAT

  • Two timed sections, Reading & Writing then Math, just like the real Digital SAT.
  • Adaptive modules that adjust to your level, just like the official College Board test.
  • Flag questions for review and cross out the answer choices you have ruled out.
  • An on-screen navigator that lets you jump to any question inside the module.

Question Bank

  • Original questions written to closely mirror real, official SAT questions in style and difficulty.
  • Full coverage of every domain and skill the Digital SAT actually tests.
  • New questions added all the time, so you never run out of fresh practice.
  • Load and practice your own custom question sets whenever you like.

Score Report

  • An instant, estimated 400 to 1600 score the moment you finish a test.
  • Your total broken down by section, so you see Reading & Writing next to Math.
  • A clear, worked explanation for every single question on the test.
  • A full answer review you can go back to any time you want.

Data Analytics

  • Your accuracy tracked skill by skill and domain by domain.
  • Your weakest areas surfaced at a glance, so nothing stays hidden.
  • Focused feedback after every test that points to what to study.
  • Always know precisely which topics to drill next to raise your score.
